SPEL: Synthetic Processes & Embodiment Labs

SPEL is dedicated to exploring the synthesis of AI and human processes, with a special focus on GenAI and human-embodied AI setups. The lab serves the community by providing a robot library, frequent events, and opportunities for collaboration in the field of synthetic processes and embodiment.
